# Usage

## Quick start

- Double-click `Pomodoro.exe` to open the window.
- Set your times (Work/Break/Long/Cycles).
- Click **Save** to persist your defaults.
- Click **Start** to begin.

## Behavior

- The timer runs continuously (even if you close the window to tray).
- Close button or `Esc` prompts:
  - **Yes** → go to tray
  - **No** → exit
  - **Cancel** → keep open

## Notifications

- When you send the app to tray, it shows a “still running in tray” notification.
- Clicking that tray notification opens the window.
